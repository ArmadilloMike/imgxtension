Top Posts

Adds a "top" button that displays the top posts from the last 30 days to the filter bar with the Hot and New selectors.

Credits: 32-Bit

Show Locked Icons

Shows icons you haven't unlocked on the settings page. Locked icons are grayed out and cannot be used. Hover over them to display their point requirement.

Credits: 32-Bit

Hard Block

Low-rated and Blocked comments no longer have a "click to show" button, completely hiding their comments. The comment count will inform you if any hard-blocked comments are on the page.

Credits: 32-Bit

Quick Create

Adds the same feature as the official Imgflip "Quick Create" extension. Right click an image to have the option to be taken to imgflip with it loaded as an uploaded meme. No need to download anything.

Custom ProfilesNew

Lets users edit their profiles by adding content to their tagline.
To edit your profile, add to your tagline with the following options, examples provided:
{font:"comic-sans", color:"#ffffff", pfp:"9geor3"}
Do not include options you don't want to use, e.g. if you only want to add a font then just put {font:"font-name"}.
Avaialable fonts are the ones listed under the "Site Fonts" feature. All fonts must be in lowercase and use "-" instead of spaces.

Credits: 32-Bit

Pinned MemechatsNew

Adds a "pin" button next to each user in your memechats. Click it to toggle them as a pinned chat, which will show up above non-pinned users.

Credits: 32-Bit
