Disable Ads

Removes ad elements whenever they appear. Not as fast or effective as a dedicated ad blocker, but good enough if you don't have one.

Flip Buttons

Re-adds imgflip's old flip button features. Lets you go to previous, next, and random posts from post pages.

Credits: 32-Bit

Top Posts

Adds a "top" button that displays the top posts from the last 30 days to the filter bar with the Hot and New selectors.

Credits: 32-Bit

Show Locked Icons

Shows icons you haven't unlocked on the settings page. Locked icons are grayed out and cannot be used. Hover over them to display their point requirement.

Credits: 32-Bit

Hard Block

Blocked users no longer have a "click to show blocked comment" button, completely hiding their comments. The comment count will inform you if any hard blocked comments are on the page.

Credits: 32-Bit

MSMG Link

Puts a button that links to MSMG in the website header, next to the home page button.

Credits: 32-Bit Meme Rag (Art)
